about us

what makes our coffee special?

Dark Sister Coffee Shop: Where Every Bean Tells a Story In the heart of Kisii County, nestled amidst the rolling hills and lush landscapes, lies a hidden gem – Dark Sister Coffee Shop. Founded by two sisters with a shared passion for coffee and community, Dark Sister is more than just a place to get your caffeine fix. It's a tribute to the rich coffee heritage of Kenya and the warmth of sisterhood. **Our Journey Begins:** Born and raised in Kisii, we grew up surrounded by the aroma of freshly roasted coffee beans. Our parents, coffee farmers, instilled in us a deep appreciation for the art of coffee-making. From the tender age of five, we would accompany them to the coffee fields, learning the delicate process of nurturing and harvesting the finest Arabica beans. **A Dream Realized:** Driven by our love for coffee and a desire to create a space where people could come together, we set out on a mission to bring the essence of our homeland to every cup. Dark Sister was born out of a dream – a dream to share the unique flavors of Kisii with the world and to create a haven where stories are shared, friendships are forged, and memories are made. **Our Commitment:** At Dark Sister, we are committed to sourcing only the finest, sustainably-grown coffee beans. Each batch is carefully roasted to perfection, ensuring that every cup delivers a symphony of flavors that dance on your palate. Our skilled baristas craft each drink with love and precision, making sure that every visit is a delightful experience. **Join Us:** Whether you're a seasoned coffee connoisseur or just beginning your coffee journey, Dark Sister welcomes you with open arms. Step into our cozy haven, savor the rich aromas, and let us take you on a sensory adventure. From the first sip to the last drop, you'll taste the passion and dedication that goes into every cup. Welcome to Dark Sister, where every bean tells a story, and every story is worth savoring.
